A business hackathon is a spinoff of the traditional Hackathon where individuals come together to create new projects in a short period of time come together to develop a viable product/service and pitch it in a weekend. HackATL is a great place to learn about a particular technology you might be interested in, meet other people who are interested in tech and entreprensurship, and also exercise your creativity and curiosity.

All current undergraduate and graduate students with a valid student ID are allowed to participate. Students who have graduated within 6 months before HackATL can also participate. While graduate students can participate in HackATL, we limit 1 graduate student per 2 undergraduate students. For example, a team of 6 may have no more than 2 graduate students. Everyone is welcome to attend speaker events and workshops.
